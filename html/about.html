About OneKey

OneKey is a student-driven organization dedicated to making a positive impact in our community through music, technology, and volunteerism. Our mission is to unlock the potential of young people and foster meaningful connections across generations.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate high school students, OneKey began as a small initiative to bring music and joy to local senior homes during the pandemic. Over time, our team has grown, and so has our impact. Today, we organize performances, tech workshops, and tutoring sessions for people of all ages.

We believe that everyone has something valuable to share, and we strive to create opportunities for students to develop their skills while giving back to the community. Our volunteers come from diverse backgrounds, united by a common goal: to make a difference, one key at a time.
